Nations Sign Historic Climate Pact

Published: July 3, 2025 | By: Green Future News

In a landmark move, 150 countries signed a climate agreement committing to net-zero emissions by 2050. This includes green infrastructure funding, carbon tax systems, and renewable energy acceleration.

Climate activists applaud the decision but stress the need for transparent implementation and enforcement.
